Privacy Policy — Stream Market

Last updated: January 2025

Stream Market ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that adds decentralized prediction-market functionality to Twitch. This Privacy Policy describes what user data the Extension handles and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect and Store

The Extension stores the following data only on your device using Chrome’s local storage (chrome.storage.local):

  • Wallet address — Your Polygon/MetaMask wallet address when you connect.
  • Wallet balance — Cached balance (POL) used to display in the UI.
  • Chain ID — The blockchain network you are connected to (e.g. Polygon).
  • Participation records — Local record of your positions and claimable outcomes for display in the Extension.

2. How We Use This Data

This data is used solely to:

  • Display your connected wallet, balance, and position history inside the Extension.
  • Interact with the Polygon blockchain and smart contracts when you participate in prediction markets or claim from the protocol (wallet address only, via your MetaMask or similar wallet).

3. Data Sharing and Transmission

We do not transmit, sell, or share your wallet address, balance, or position history with any third-party servers, advertisers, or data brokers. All of the data listed above remains on your device.

The Extension may communicate with:

  • Twitch — To load Twitch pages and show prediction UI.
  • Polygon RPC / Polygonscan — To read and submit blockchain transactions when you use prediction features.
  • CoinGecko — To fetch POL price for display (no personal data sent).

4. Data Storage and Retention

All stored data is kept only in your browser’s extension storage. It is removed when you uninstall the Extension. We do not retain any copies of your data on our own servers.

5. Your Choices

You can disconnect your wallet and clear stored data by uninstalling the Extension. Disconnecting in the Extension’s UI stops further use of your wallet address for that session.

6. Compliance

Our use of user data complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy and Limited Use requirements. We limit use of data to providing and improving the Extension’s single purpose.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or the Extension, please use the support contact provided on the Chrome Web Store listing.
